## What the class covers

Home Assistant is the **middleman** that connects all your smart devices and protocols (Zigbee, Z-Wave, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth) into one local app. No ten vendor apps, no cloud lock-in, your data stays yours. Over ~2.5 hours the class goes from "what is this?" to running automations, choosing protocols and gear, and even letting an AI agent build your dashboards.
